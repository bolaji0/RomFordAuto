Our Services

Comprehensive auto repair and maintenance services

MOT Testing

Certified MOT testing to ensure your vehicle meets all legal requirements and safety standards.

Our DVSA-approved MOT testing center provides comprehensive inspections for all vehicle types. Our experienced testers conduct thorough checks of your vehicle's safety features, emissions, and overall roadworthiness.

  • Class 1 & 2 - Motorcycles
  • Class 4 - Cars & light vans
  • Class 5 - Private passenger vehicles
  • Class 7 - Commercial vehicles up to 3,500kg

MOT tests typically take 45-60 minutes, and we offer same-day appointments.

Engine Diagnostics

Advanced computer diagnostics to identify and troubleshoot engine issues with precision.

Our state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment can identify issues across all major vehicle systems, providing accurate fault codes and recommendations.

  • Engine management system diagnostics
  • Electronic control unit (ECU) diagnostics
  • Emissions system testing
  • Performance and efficiency analysis
  • Check engine light investigation

We provide a detailed report explaining all findings and recommended repairs.

Brake Repairs

Complete brake system maintenance and repairs for optimal safety and performance.

Our brake specialists provide comprehensive services to ensure your vehicle's braking system functions safely and efficiently.

  • Brake pad and shoe replacement
  • Disc and drum brake service
  • Brake fluid flush and replacement
  • Brake caliper rebuilding or replacement
  • ABS system diagnosis and repair
  • Emergency brake adjustment

We use high-quality parts that meet or exceed manufacturer specifications for lasting performance.

Exhaust Systems

Comprehensive exhaust system repairs, including catalytic converters and mufflers.

Our exhaust system services ensure optimal engine performance, fuel efficiency, and compliance with emissions standards.

  • Complete exhaust system inspection
  • Muffler repair and replacement
  • Catalytic converter diagnostics and replacement
  • Exhaust pipe repair and replacement
  • Emissions control system service
  • Performance exhaust upgrades

We offer warranties on all exhaust work and use quality materials designed to resist corrosion.

Clutch Repairs

Expert clutch diagnosis, maintenance, and replacement for manual transmission vehicles.

Our clutch repair services address all aspects of manual transmission clutch systems for smoother gear changes and improved drivability.

  • Clutch replacement
  • Clutch master and slave cylinder repair
  • Flywheel resurfacing or replacement
  • Clutch cable adjustment or replacement
  • Hydraulic line service
  • Transmission leak repairs

We provide guidance on driving habits to maximize clutch lifespan after repairs.

General Maintenance

Regular maintenance services to keep your vehicle running at peak performance.

Our comprehensive maintenance services help prevent costly repairs and extend your vehicle's lifespan.

  • Oil and filter changes
  • Fluid checks and top-ups
  • Air and cabin filter replacement
  • Tire rotation and balancing
  • Battery testing and replacement
  • Belt and hose inspection
  • Light and electrical system checks
  • Wiper blade replacement

We offer maintenance packages tailored to your vehicle's make, model, and driving conditions.
